About Me

My name is Sharon and I am a front-end developer who studied at the African Developers Academy for a year, with a background in Electronic Engineering and Arduino programming.

I am a fast learner easy to work with and I am a team player. When working on a project I always plan how we are going to reach the target and make something great. I am passionate about coding, digital technology and empowering young people in disadvantaged communities.

more about me

I am an ADA Graduate!

I have just finished studying Front-End Web Development at the African Developers Academy. When I started I didn't know any coding languages, but it was a great experience for me. This is especially true because I am always looking for opportunities which relate to the Fourth Industrial Revolution as we are now in a digital world where most things are done electronically.

I am now familiar with coding languages like HTML, CSS and Javascript as well as the use of API’s. I also learned how to create mobile responsive designs and I often use the Bootstrap 4 framework.
